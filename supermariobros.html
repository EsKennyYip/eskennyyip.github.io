Super Mario Bros
Java Awt Swing

A Recreation of the entire first level of Super Mario Bros platformer game developed from scratch using Java and the built-in awt/swing graphics. Sounds, sprites, game physics and mechanics implemented have been implemented with about 95% accuracy to the original game. I modified the game so that the player can alternatingly play as Mario and Luigi. Mario/Luigi can change between mini, normal, and fire flower forms. The invincibility star is also available. Interactions with goombas and koopas are the same as the original game. Jumping on the goomba's head or striking a brick that a goomba is standing on will knock it out. Stepping on the koopa will cause it to hide in its shell, after which, stepping on it again will kick it in a direction. The secret underground pipe map is also included. The game took me 6 days, 12 hours each day, to complete.
