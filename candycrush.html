Candy Crush
HTML CSS JavaScript Node.js Express MongoDB

A Candy Crush game, similar in functionality to the original, built with Node.js, Express, JavaScript, and designed with HTML, CSS. The project includes several algorithms that determin all possibile candy create and crush combinations. Players have 20 turns to try and get the highest score they possible can and at the end, may register their score. A leaderboard of player and scores are displayed at the end of the game.
